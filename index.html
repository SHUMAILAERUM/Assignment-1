White Chicken Karahi

  1. 1kg Chicken
  2. 100ml Yougurt
  3. 100ml Cream
  4. 50g salted butter
  5. Freshly grated ginger
  6. Shan masala and crushed Black pepper and corriender to taste

Method

  • Chop chicken into medium pieces
  • Simmer oil add ginger garlic paste with chiecken, fry them for 2 minutes
  • Add Shan masala pack yougurt and cream
  • cook in medium flame until tendor
  • sprinkle green corriender, a pinch of balck papper, ginger and green chili
  • serve with naan
